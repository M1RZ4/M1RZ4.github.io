About Me

My name is Mirza Ojeda Veira and I am from Gijón, Asturias. I have always loved everything related with software and technology. That's the reason why I decided to study Software Engineering and turned coding into my passion.
In the last years I have taken a step forward to fulfill my dream of working as a Game Programmer, finishing my Master's degree at the Complutense University of Madrid and working on some independent projects to improve my skills and create amazing games. In the last years I've been working as a full time Gameplay Programmer, first on Limitless Games and then on DR Studios. Right now I'm looking for a new position as Gameplay Programmer.
I've worked with many different technologies across all these years, but I'm more used to work with Unreal Engine 4 and C++, although I'm open to work with any tech.
