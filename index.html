About Me

My name is Mirza Ojeda Veira and I am from Gijón, Asturias, but now I live in Barcelona. I have always loved everything related with software and technology. That's the reason why I decided to study Software Engineering and find my passion in coding.
In the last years I have taken a step forward to fulfill my dream of working as a Game Programmer, finishing my Master's degree at the Complutense University of Madrid and working on some independent projects to improve my skills and create amazing games. In last months I've been working as a full time Gameplay Programmer in Limitless Games, developing the game Ascendants Rising, but now I'm looking for new challenges.
I've worked with many different technologies across all these years but I'm more used to work with Unreal Engine 4 and C++, and recently I've developed some knowledge on the Gameplay Ability System of the same engine, but I'm open to work with new technologies.
